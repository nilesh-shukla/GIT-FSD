ABES Engineering College

ABES Engineering College, located in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, is a renowned institution committed to providing quality education in the fields of engineering, technology, and management. Established in 2000, the college is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

The college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, particularly known for its Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) courses in various specializations such as Computer Science, Information Technology, Electronics, and Mechanical Engineering. The institution also has an MBA program aimed at nurturing future business leaders.

ABES is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, including well-equipped laboratories, libraries, and modern classrooms. The faculty comprises experienced professionals and academicians dedicated to fostering a learning environment that emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

The institution promotes holistic development through its focus on extracurricular activities, including technical clubs, cultural events, and sports. ABES also encourages innovation and entrepreneurship through its E-Cell (Entrepreneurship Cell), which helps students transform their ideas into viable business ventures.

Moreover, ABES Engineering College is known for its strong placement record, with students securing job offers from leading companies across various industries.
